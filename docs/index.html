About Bookshelf Manager


Bookshelf Manager is a vertical social network, dedicated to book lovers and all readers. Bookshelf Manager is the richest source of information on books and the most independent. Sign up and search Bookshelf Manager to find useful, interesting information on millions of books, share opinions with other readers, and see what other think by reading their reviews and opinions.

In a nutshell, Bookshelf Manager is where book lovers call home.

It is a place where you can see what your friends are reading and vice versa. You can create "bookshelves" to organize what you've read (or want to read). You can comment on each other's reviews. You can find your next favorite book. And on this journey with your friends you can explore new territory, gather information, and expand your mind.

Knowledge is power, and power is best shared among readers.

The application was written in SPA technology using the vue.js framework. The visual page was created with vutefiy. Firebase is responsible for the backend page, with which users can create accounts and also share data on books using Firestore.

Free to use

Our app requires no payments. Sign in and use without any additional fees and charges.

No Ads

Experience the convenient interface of our ad-free application. Enjoy full access to your bookshelf without any interruptions.

Responsive

Use your bookshelf whenever you want and from what device you want. The application is fully adapted to mobile devices and desktops.

What are the most important features of our app?

  • Build and update your profile:
    • Register
    • Sign in
    • Log out
  • Create your shelves and your wish list:
    • Add books to "bookshelf"
    • Change status to:
      • Reading
      • Want to read
    • Search via genre
  • Track the books you're reading and want to read.
  • Review and rate books that you read:
    • Add comments
    • Add rates
    • Add to favourites
  • Check out your personalized book recommendations:
    • Find a book for you from the recommended books
    • Find the book from all books
  • Find out if a book is a good fit for you from our community’s reviews.
  • Expand the list of all books.

Bookshelf Manager Gallery

Login panel

You can create your own account and log in using your email and password.

Main view

After logging in to the application you will see a list of the most popular books, and you will also receive recommendations for books to read.

All books

From the view of all books, you can search for books using the search engine, and add them to "Bookshelf" using a special icon.

Book view

By clicking on the selected book you will receive information about its author, publication date, number of pages, cover picture, as well as a description of the book. Here you will also see a review of other users, and you can add your own comments and likes.

Add new Book

The application also allows you to add new books.

User account

Going to the user account you will see three tabs: Bookshelf, Want to read and Reading. After expanding them, you will receive a list of books that you have added to Bookshelf. From here, you can transfer them to the Want to read and Reading list using special icons. You can rate books on the Reading list.

Accounts of other users

You can browse other users' book lists and discover new books.

About subpage

A description of the application and its most important functionalities is available on the About subpage. Here you can also check the number of reviews and books added in the application.

Book genres

By choosing a specific genre from the menu you will receive a list of books of that genre. The photo shows an example of books from the genre of "Business".
